A Quick Fork in the Job Road

This year I switched jobs. Continue reading to learn a bit more about what I'm doing now.

December 12, 2023 PSUStevens

3 minutes read

 Twitter

 LinkedIn

 Email

 Bluesky

Let’s put it this way. I wasn’t looking for a new job. But due to an unforeseen curveball, I now work for Pure Storage. This wasn’t a planned move, but looking back (6 months now). I have no complaints!! I wish all of my friends and colleagues at Entrust the best of luck and I know we will see each other at future trade shows or somewhere else along our travels.

What am I doing now?

My official title is: Field Solutions Architect or FSA. As an FSA my team and I are specialists across several different subject areas, including virtualization (VMware, Hyper-V, OpenStack, etc.), databases (MS SQL, Oracle, SAP), cloud (Azure, AWS) etc. I am a specialist in the virtualization and cloud areas. I work everyday with our North American Systems Engineering (SE) teams when their customers/prospects would like to go a bit into the weeds on both of my specialty areas. I will be writing about each of those areas and some of the Pure goodness in future articles right here.

Tell me more

The team I joined at Pure is nothing but top notch folks. We have all been in the industry for many years and the collaboration between the entire team is nothing like I have ever experienced before. There are no wrong questions to ask. Do we give each other a hard time every now and then? Of Course! I wouldn’t expect anything less. However, when challenges are presented to one or more of us on an opportunity we come together as a cohesive unit.

Wrap up

To wrap up this article, I can’t say enough good things about Pure and the team I joined. I landed at a fantastic place to work. I’ll be continuing to talk about many virtualization topics, mostly related to storage and some not related to storage. So, please stay tuned.
